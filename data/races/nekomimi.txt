Ability Score Increase: Your dexterity increases by 2

Age: Nekomimi mature at the same rate as humans but live a few years shorter.

Alignment: Nekomimi do not have a preferred alignment, however most tend to be neutral or good as those are much cuter alignments.

Size: Given half of their ancestry is human, Nekomimi stand about 5'0 to 6'0 and are medium sized.

Darkvision: Nekomimi have superior vision in dark and dim conditions. You can see dim light within 60 feet of you as if it were bright light and in darkness as if it were dim light. You can't disern color in darkness, only 50 shades of gray.

Sharp claws: You can deal 1d4 slashing damage unarmed. This damage is melee and counts as a light weapon.

Hunter's prowess: Nekomimi are gifted hunters and have a natural +1 bonus to sneak and can move 5ft faster than other medium creatures.

Animal Ancestry: You can commune with other cat like creatures

Languages: You can speak, read, and write Common and speak in purrs, growls, and meows.
